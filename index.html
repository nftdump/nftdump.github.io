NFT Dump

NFT Dump is a research repository of work completed by Michelle Kasprzak in 2021-2023 on the subject of non-fungible tokens. Start here with an essay exploring the phenomenon of NFTs, and the debate around their environmental impact and utility as tools for activism: "Ethical Engagement with NFTs: Impossibility or Viable Aspiration?"
A subsequent major piece of work was The Crypto Purse, a collaboration with lizvlx of UBERMORGEN.COM.
